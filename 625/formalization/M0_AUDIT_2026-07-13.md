## Independent adversarial reviews

### Graph semantics and gluing — PASS

The reviewer checked that cocolouring fibers form the intended partition,
empty colors mean “at most `k` nonempty parts,” and the empty-vertex and
zero-palette cases are coherent.  The induced-set gluing proof correctly uses
disjoint palettes, so it needs no cross-edge hypothesis.  Adding empty colors,
monotonicity, and the minimum characterization are non-circular.
`Nonempty.some` only extracts an already-proved witness; existence of the
cochromatic minimum comes from the trivial finite proper coloring.

### Probability target — PASS

The reviewer checked the exact constant, natural-log convention, real rather
than truncated subtraction, the `G(n,1/2)` law, its uniform singleton mass,
event measurability, and full-sequence convergence.  The `ENNReal` proposition
is proved equivalent to the manuscript's real-valued probability limit.
Totalized values at `n=0,1` form a harmless finite prefix.  No coupling between
different `n` is required for the marginal-probability statement.

### Reproducibility and trust — PASS with the CI caveat above

The reviewer matched every dependency checkout to its manifest commit,
confirmed root-module coverage, replayed `lake build --wfail`, checked the
axiom output and token scans, and confirmed that generated artifacts are
ignored.  The mutable nanoda/helper finding was repaired before this verdict.
